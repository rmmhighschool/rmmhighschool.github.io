Summary About Us

Quality education can groom children to be confident civilians capable of coping up in the highly competitive world. RMM English High School was founded in 2006 to give underprivileged children an access to high quality education at a very low cost. With only 2 classrooms, 4 teachers and 33 students back then to the flourishing 7-story infrastructure today, RMMHS houses a playgroup, Pre-primary, primary and secondary school with dedicated library, a science lab and computer lab within the premises. All classrooms are equipped with a television to facilitate e-learning and storytelling methods that make learning fun. Besides, education field trips, cultural programmes, festive celebrations, sports day and annual days are celebrated for their all-round development.
